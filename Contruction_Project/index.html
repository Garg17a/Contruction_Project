About Us

Vugar Limited is a premier construction company located in Port Moresby, Papua New Guinea. With 22 years of experience in the industry, we deliver high-quality construction projects that meet international standards.

Our journey began over two decades ago with a mission to transform the construction landscape of Papua New Guinea. Since our inception, we have grown into a trusted name known for our commitment to excellence, innovation, and sustainability.

Our Services

  • Residential Construction

    We specialize in building high-quality residential properties, including single-family homes, townhouses, and apartments. Our team ensures each project is completed with meticulous attention to detail and superior craftsmanship.

  • Commercial Construction

    Our commercial construction services cater to businesses of all sizes. From office buildings and retail spaces to warehouses and industrial facilities, we deliver projects that meet your business needs and exceed your expectations.

  • Infrastructure Development

    We are experts in infrastructure development, including roads, bridges, and utilities. Our projects are designed to improve community connectivity and support economic growth in Papua New Guinea.

  • Renovation and Remodeling

    Whether you're looking to renovate a single room or remodel an entire building, our team provides comprehensive renovation services. We work closely with clients to transform their spaces into functional and aesthetically pleasing environments.
